Blue Light Card: User Feedback Dashboard

An interactive summary of user sentiment and key engagement metrics.

Key Findings Summary

The feedback indicates a mixed user experience. While the digital membership card and the general concept of appreciation are highly valued, users frequently struggle with finding relevant discounts due to poor search functionality and a lack of personalisation. Technical glitches and slow loading times also hinder the experience. The data suggests a strong desire for a more tailored and efficient app experience.

Conclusion & Strategic Recommendations

The Blue Light Card app provides a valued service, particularly through its digital card and the sense of appreciation it conveys. To significantly increase user engagement and daily active usage, the product team should prioritise enhancing personalisation and search capabilities to make discount discovery effortless. Addressing technical performance issues and streamlining the redemption process (especially for cross-device use) will also be critical for fostering a more habitual and satisfying user experience.

Favourite Features: Detailed Themes

  • Digital Card: The convenience of having the card on their phone is a major highlight.
  • QR Code Discounts: Users find QR codes easy and simple to use for in-store redemptions.
  • Flexibility: The ability to use discounts both online and in-store is highly valued.
  • Auto-Emailed Codes: The feature that automatically emails discount codes is seen as a convenient touch.

Feeling Appreciated: Detailed Themes

  • Positive Recognition: Many users explicitly state they "feel appreciated" and see it as a "nice perk" and a form of recognition for their service.
  • Mixed Feelings on Value: Some feel the appreciation is diminished by small discount percentages (e.g., 3-4%) which they feel are not significant.
  • Generalised vs. Targeted: The sentiment of appreciation is lower when offers feel too general or not specifically targeted to their profession or interests.

Top Frustrations: Detailed Themes

  • Search Functionality: Users are frustrated by long, irrelevant search results and a lack of effective filtering options.
  • Technical Glitches: The app is described as "glitchy," with frequent logouts and UI bugs (e.g., search bar hiding).
  • Performance: Slow loading times for the home screen images and search function are common complaints.
  • Connectivity Issues: Users report problems when they have poor internet connections.
  • Unclear Redemption: Confusion around when a link must be used to get a discount online.

Most Requested Changes: Detailed Themes

  • Personalisation: The most frequent request is for tailored offers, personalised homepages ("like Netflix"), and suggestions based on usage history.
  • Favourites & Notifications: Users want to save their favourite retailers and receive notifications when new offers from them are available.
  • Improved Search & Filters: A direct request for better search functionality with more comprehensive filtering.
  • Biometric Login: A desire for easier and faster login using biometrics.
  • Cross-Device Usability: A better way to use codes on a laptop that are generated in the mobile app (e.g., "vanity codes").
